Market Rollout, Reuse, and Copilot Utilization

• Market rollout assumptions Typical markets will have 70–80% “bluegrade/boilerplate” reuse and 20–30% net-new design. Once one market is implemented, subsequent markets should be faster via reuse.

• Developer efficiency with Copilot Expectation to demonstrate maturity in leveraging GitHub Copilot (or similar) to reduce repetitive work and avoid 10–15 days of reimplementation per market for reusable parts.

• Componentization approach Components should be built to work across grids. During development, mock data can be used for cross-grid components; integration will be done grid-wise with real APIs.
